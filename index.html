Dr Ellie Pryor.

Researcher

I am dedicated to understanding present-day South African climate and applying this knowledge to reconstruct it’s past climate during the last glacial cycle using a multi-proxy approach. The work linked land-sea climate changes to Homo sapiens cultural evolution during the time interval of 100-50 thousand years ago.
